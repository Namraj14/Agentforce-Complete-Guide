# Why Agentforce?

Traditional automation has limitations.

## Traditional Automation

Examples:

- Validation Rules
- Process Builder
- Flow
- Einstein Bots

Challenges:

- Rule-based
- Limited flexibility
- Difficult maintenance
- Cannot reason

## Agentforce Advantages

Agentforce can:

- Understand natural language
- Reason about requests
- Select appropriate actions
- Work across multiple systems

## Business Benefits

### Improved Customer Experience

Faster responses.

### Increased Productivity

Reduces manual work.

### 24x7 Availability

Supports customers anytime.

### Reduced Operational Cost

Fewer repetitive tasks handled by agents.

## Example

Traditional Bot:

"Choose option 1, 2 or 3"

Agentforce:

"Tell me how I can help you today."
