# Tokens

LLMs do not read words.

They read Tokens.

## Example

Sentence:

Hello World

Tokens:

"Hello"
"World"

## Example

Sentence:

I love Salesforce

Tokens:

I
love
Salesforce

## Why Tokens Matter

Token usage impacts:

- Cost
- Performance
- Response Length

## Agentforce Interview Question

Why should prompts be concise?

Answer:

Because unnecessary text increases token consumption and cost.
